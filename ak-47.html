Despite the ferocious name, AK-47 is a relaxing and mellowing strain. Although it is sativa dominant strain that packs an 65:35 sativa/indica ratio; you can look forward to a long lasting cerebral buzz upon your first few tokes. The fusion of Thai, Mexican, Afghani, and Colombian varieties make AK-47's scent strong and sour. In addition to its vivid lime green color, you can expect to see long and sticky orange hairs once you get your hands on this amazing bud. Patients favor this strain because of its ability to alleviate pain and induce relaxation; so if you are suffering from anxiety, stress, depression or pain, this strain is for you! AK-47 can walk the walk for the reputation that it has been given. This strain has won many awards for its high THC content including the Cannabis Cup Created in 1992 by Serious Seeds, AK-47 has won numerous Cannabis Cup awards around the world for its soaring THC content. For those hoping to fill their gardens with this resinous, skunky hybrid, growers recommend an indoor environment with either soil or hydroponic setups. AK-47 is easy to grow and has a short indoor flowering time of just 53 to 63 days, while outdoor plants typically finish toward the end of October.

AK-47 was one of those buds that you heard about all the time back in the day. Living on the east coast you barely ever saw it. It became somewhat of a legend in my head. Let’s focus on today when you can get good weed everywhere. I got an 1/8th of AK and my batch was cured well, but was dry. I got it on the black market, so that’s the chance you take. I rolled up a blunt in a natural wrap and I used the AK-47 as my wake n bake as it’s Sativa heavy. After 2 hits I instantly felt the pressure behind my eyes release. My head was clear and my body relaxed. The flavor was what I remembered a sweet Pine taste that leaves a real nice sweet taste on your tongue after the exhale. As a wake n bake this is a great smoke. The buzz is relaxing and your mind is free. You can do nothing or get into a project or run errands. This stain is great for pain caused by inflammation. My wife has an auto immune and this helps greatly with the pain. I give it a four as I like the Indica punch more than the Sativa lift.
